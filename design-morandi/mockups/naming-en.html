Nesio · Naming (EN)宝盒 英文命名
Same 3 rules, tuned to how English UI reads: actions are one strong verb, sections are one plain noun, voice stays warm. Primary pick in bold, alt where a second option is worth a look.
Rule 1 · Actions
One verb, imperative
Snap / Say / Keep — same length, same grammar.
Rule 2 · Sections
Plain noun, no jargon
"Insights," never "Cognition." Reads at a glance.
Rule 3 · Voice
From the box's side
"Keep" it for you — not the OS word "Share."
